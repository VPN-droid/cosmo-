Frequently Asked Questions

Why do some games show a blank screen?

Some schools use deep packet inspection that blocks websites from loading inside iframes. If this happens, use the Cloak Tab button to bypass the filter.

What is the Panic Key?

By pressing the tilde key (`~`), the screen instantly transforms into a graphing calculator to hide your activity. Press it again to return.

What does Transport mean?

It's the method our backend proxy uses to fetch data. Libcurl and Bare are standard. If sites aren't loading, trying switching it.
